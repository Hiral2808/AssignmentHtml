ABOUT US
PolyOne is the first arts and entertainment NFT platform with a Global Mission. We empower an international roster of creators and organizations across the spectrum of artistic mediums. With our strategic partners Trendland and Braw Haus, we curate discerning collections of digital art and bring exciting installations to public venues. We offer tips and tools for new creators to break into NFTs through our NFT Incubator space which is a dynamic hub for collaborations, classes or lively crypto/NFT gatherings. Our flexible smart contracts and white label solutions will allow film studios, Broadway shows, dance companies, musical groups, designers or other arts organization to seamlessly enter this new artistic domain. And our layered-NFT approach peels back and celebrates each individual art form required for a collaborative work. With PolyOne, all creators and collectors make a difference. One hundred percent of PolyOne proceeds benefit Global One, a defi organization which finances private-sector ESG companies that either elevate people's standards of living or address matters of global concern. We believe all art tells a story. Ours. Of many, One. PolyOne. Create to elevate
